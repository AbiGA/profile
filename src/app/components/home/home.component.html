TIP is home to clean, contemporary design. We work with you to understand your business, needs and goals, and deploy our design expertise to help your brand get to where it should be. We believe in clarity in messaging, to attract and retain the right kind of attention. We cut through the clutter in today’s over-saturated marketing landscape through effective and intuitive design.

At TIP, we are a lean, efficient team, with a wide range of expertise, that brings you bold, intuitive design, that is backed by a solid foundation of research. Work with us for smart, direct and collaborative design and marketing that helps you carve out the right space for your brand.

TIP was set up with the aim of making clean, modern design accessible to all, especially in the fast-maturing Indian market. Having studied and worked in design and marketing in the US, founder and CEO, Divya Hegde founded TIP with the aim to plug the vacuum for good contemporary design in India. TIP focuses on uncompromising quality, intuitive and purposeful design, based on contemporary design principles and navigating digital marketing based on a keen practical awareness of the digital landscape

At TIP, we believe that design is not just for companies, but for society. TIP Sessions is an integral part of our two-pronged strategy to spark change in social attitudes, where we leverage our skills and privilege as young, urban designers to make a real, measurable impact in society, on issues that matter.

TIP Sessions is a forum for action on local issues that are addressed through intelligent design and targeted, local action. The goal is to bring together relevant stakeholders - be they government officials, academics, NGOs, expert bodies, and most importantly, the community - to facilitate a meaningful exchange of information and solutions that are actionable at the local level that create exponential knock-on effect in the community. Read More

Our ongoing initiative, TIP Sessions: Coastal Edit focuses on issues that affect communities in Coastal Karnataka, including water conservation, climate change, sustainable fishing practices, etc. while researching and working to introduce practices at the individual and community level that help to tilt the delicate ecological balance in Karnataka’s coastal belt towards greater sustainability.
